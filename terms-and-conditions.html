MOTRADES POLICIES

TERMS & CONDITIONS

Please review our policies carefully before participating in live breaks, purchasing products, or using the Motrades platform and services.

REFUND POLICY

All sales are final once a live break has been completed.

Due to the nature of sports card products, sealed hobby boxes and break spots cannot be refunded or returned after products have been opened during a live break.

Before purchasing any product or break spot, customers are responsible for reviewing:

  • Break details

  • Product descriptions

  • Team assignments

  • Shipping information

  • Break schedules

By completing a purchase on Motrades, you acknowledge and agree to these terms.

SHIPPING POLICY

All orders are shipped to the address entered during checkout.

To maintain secure payment processing and order verification:

  • Orders must ship to the submitted billing/shipping address

  • Customers are responsible for maintaining accurate account information

  • Address updates should be completed before placing an order

WEEKLY SHIPPING SCHEDULE

Motrades processes shipments weekly to provide:

  • Consistent delivery scheduling

  • Improved packaging efficiency

  • Safer shipment handling

  • Reduced shipping delays

Tracking information will be sent to the email address connected to your account once shipping has been processed.

WHAT CARDS SHIP?

Unless stated otherwise in the break listing, shipped cards may include:

  • Autographs

  • Memorabilia cards

  • Serial Numbered Cards

  • Inserts

  • Rookie Cards

  • Major Hits

Some premium releases may also include base cards depending on the product type.

Non-shipped base cards may be donated to:

  • Schools

  • Youth organizations

  • Community programs

  • Hospitals

COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Motrades is built for collectors who share a passion for sports cards and hobby culture.

Any member violating community standards may have their account suspended or permanently removed.
