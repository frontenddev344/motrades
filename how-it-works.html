UNDERSTANDING LIVE GROUP BREAKS

Learn how sports card group breaks work, how cards are assigned, and what to expect when participating in live breaks on Motrades.

👥

WHAT IS A GROUP BREAK?

A group break is when multiple collectors join together to split the cost of opening sports card hobby boxes or premium cases.

This allows collectors to enjoy premium products at a more affordable price while still having the opportunity to pull rare cards, autographs, rookie cards, and major hobby hits.

💲

HOW MUCH DO GROUP BREAKS COST?

  • Product type
  • Hobby box value
  • Number of spots available
  • Sport and player demand
  • Break format

Premium products with high rookie potential or limited releases may carry higher pricing compared to standard hobby products.

📦

HOW ARE CARDS ASSIGNED?

For team-based breaks, cards are assigned based on the official team logo displayed on the card.

Additional factors such as jersey colors, college uniforms, or memorabilia colors do not determine ownership.

  1. Current professional team
  2. Team with most games played
  3. Drafted team (if player never appeared professionally)
