HOW MOTRADES WORKS

UNDERSTANDING LIVE GROUP BREAKS

Learn how sports card group breaks work, how cards are assigned, and what to expect when participating in live breaks on Motrades.

👥

WHAT IS A GROUP BREAK?

A group break is when multiple collectors join together to split the cost of opening sports card hobby boxes or premium cases.

This allows collectors to enjoy premium products at a more affordable price while still having the opportunity to pull rare cards, autographs, rookie cards, and major hobby hits.

💲

HOW MUCH DO GROUP BREAKS COST?

  • Product type
  • Hobby box value
  • Number of spots available
  • Sport and player demand
  • Break format

Premium products with high rookie potential or limited releases may carry higher pricing compared to standard hobby products.

📦

HOW ARE CARDS ASSIGNED?

For team-based breaks, cards are assigned based on the official team logo displayed on the card.

Additional factors such as jersey colors, college uniforms, or memorabilia colors do not determine ownership.

  1. Current professional team
  2. Team with most games played
  3. Drafted team (if player never appeared professionally)

TYPES OF GROUP BREAKS

PICK YOUR TEAM BREAK

Collectors choose and purchase specific teams they want in the break. Team pricing may vary depending on product strength and rookie potential.

RANDOM TEAM BREAK

Every participant pays the same price and receives a randomly assigned team once the break is filled.

SERIAL NUMBER BREAK

Collectors receive random numbers between 0–9. Any card ending with that serial number belongs to the assigned participant.

Example:A card numbered 24/99 would belong to the collector assigned #4.

DIVISIONAL BREAK

Participants receive a random division instead of a single team, increasing the number of possible cards they may receive.

RANDOM HIT BREAK

Each purchased spot guarantees a random hit, including autographs, memorabilia cards, premium inserts, and rare chase cards. All hits are distributed randomly among participants, giving every collector a fair and equal opportunity to receive exciting and high-value cards.

HOW ARE MULTI-TEAM CARDS HANDLED?

When cards feature multiple teams or players, a randomization process is used to determine ownership.

Weighted randomization may apply depending on how many players or teams appear on the card.

For major high-value cards, participants may also choose to:

💰 Sell and split proceeds
🤝 Arrange a private buyout
🎯 Proceed with randomization

If no agreement is reached, randomization will be used.

3x

RANDOMIZATION PROCESS

All randomizations are performed fairly during the live stream.

Randoms are typically completed multiple times to ensure balanced and transparent results.

SHIPPING & PROCEDURES

WHAT CARDS SHIP?

Unless stated otherwise in the break listing, shipped cards may include:

  • ✔ Autographs
  • ✔ Memorabilia cards
  • ✔ Rookie cards
  • ✔ Numbered parallels
  • ✔ Inserts
  • ✔ Major hits

Some standard base cards may not ship depending on product type.

WHEN ARE CARDS SHIPPED?

Orders are processed through weekly shipping cycles to improve packaging efficiency and shipment security.

Tracking details are typically sent to the email address connected to your account once orders are processed.

WHERE ARE ORDERS SHIPPED?

All packages are shipped directly to the shipping address entered during checkout.

Collectors are responsible for ensuring shipping details are accurate before submitting orders.

CARD PROTECTION & PACKAGING

Card protection is a top priority during shipping.

Premium cards are carefully secured using penny sleeves, top loaders, team bags, protective cases, and reinforced packaging materials. High-value cards are given extra protection to ensure they arrive safely and in excellent condition.
